Best Places To Go

Singapore

Popular tourist attractions include the Singapore Zoo, River Safari and Night Safari, which allows people to explore Asian, African and American habitats at night without any visible barriers between guests and the wild animals.

Philippines

The country is a biodiversity hotspot, having the world's highest endemism rate for bird species, and one of the highest for mammals and flora.

Manali

The winters cover the place with a sheet of white snow, making it all the more stunning. One can partake in sightseeing and adventure sports like river rafting, paragliding,zip lining and zorbing in summers.
