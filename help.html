1. Click on "show package" Menu and in which you have to select Any one category(Summer, winter, Monsoon).
  2. Now after open any category you will see multiple trip packages with their name and amount and days.
  3. After that whenever if you click on any one of name(eg. manali, shimla, etc) then you dicrectly rediret to the another page where you will see some pictures regarding that trip.
  4. Now go back to Main Menu and click on "Trip Booking" menu and fill up that form according to your selected trip package.
  5. After submitting the trip form then go to "Hotel Booking" menu and fill that form if you want to book hotel earlier.
  6. Now your trip have been succesfullly submitted to the trip manager.
  7. Now when your trip becomes starting then in "Expense" menu you have to add your amounts which spent on different expenses so your money handling would be easy.
  8. In "Show Expense Details" menu, it will display all the expenses which you had spent during your trip.
